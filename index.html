About Me

I'm an IT manager in the democracy and governance space, supporting people and infrastructure as well as providing comprehensive digital security trainings for any audience.

I'm also an audio engineer by hobby. I've scored a few independent films, but I'm probably best known for my interest in the theme and music of classic Doctor Who. I co-created Doctor Who: The Theme, aiming to be the definitive reference guide to the original theme.
